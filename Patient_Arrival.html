Patient Arrival Instructions:

Arrive 10 mins before the appointment
Bring photo ID and insurance card at every appointment.
Bring co-pay (if any)
Please carry any medical records/medications
Late arrival protocol?
 Any patient that arrives 10 minutes after their scheduled appointment time will be rescheduled.
