Scheduling protocol

General instructions – All providers

 11:30 appointments should only be FU’s or Walk-in (WI’s).
 New Patients (NP) will not have a chart – direct all these calls to office and send a triage to V1 Triage
 For all surgical clearance: Name of surgeon, ICD10 code, date of surgery is needed. Please have the pt/facility fax over a surgical request form to Brighton.
 For WI’s – Always mention brief description of what the pt is coming for. Eg: coug, back pain, cold etc.
 If no appointment available, transfer back to the office or send triage to V1 Triage and office staff will reach out and schedule. DO NOT add slots or make changes to the template.
  **Sick visits – send triage to V1
 **Hospital follow up should be booked within 7 days of discharge. If no slots available, triage to the V1 triage
 Labs are scheduled the week before to get the lab done and reviewed at the visit. Send Triage to V1 if the pt wants to get labs elsewhere, mention the facility name, fax number for the script to be sent
